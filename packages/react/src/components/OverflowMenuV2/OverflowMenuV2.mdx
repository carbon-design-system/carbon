## Overview

`OverflowMenuV2` is the planned successor of `OverflowMenu` and supports all [subcomponents of `Menu`](/docs/components-menu--playground#subcomponents).

```jsx
<OverflowMenuV2 label="Primary action">
  <MenuItem label="Stop app" />
  <MenuItem label="Restart app" />
  <MenuItem label="Rename app" />
  <MenuItem label="Edit routes and access" />
  <MenuItemDivider />
  <MenuItem label="Delete app" kind="danger" />
</OverflowMenuV2>
```
